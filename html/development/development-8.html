Game Together

The primary goal of this project was to create a web application called Game Together, designed to help gamers form groups for their favorite games. This app allows users to post game offers to meet and play on specific dates and times, create online groups, add extra players to their team, and communicate via chat to facilitate collaboration. The aim was to build a platform where like-minded gamers could easily connect and form communities.

Things I Learned and Applied:

Backend Development:

Utilized HTML, EJS, CSS, JavaScript, and Svelte to design and implement the backend architecture, supporting user authentication, group creation, and real-time chat functionalities. This project marked my first experience working with backend technologies.

Feature Implementation:

Developed a "liking" feature, allowing users to create lists of their favorite games. This enabled other gamers to see which games and platforms users preferred, making it easier to find compatible players for specific games like Tekken on PC.

Concept Development:

I created the Game Together concept to address the challenge of finding suitable players for gaming groups. The app was inspired by my own hobby of gaming and aimed to create a game-exclusive social platform similar to Reddit or Discord, focused on forming gaming parties and fostering gaming communities.

User Experience and Interface Design:

Designed an intuitive and user-friendly interface, ensuring that users could easily navigate the app and find relevant information about other gamers, such as their game preferences, platforms, playing times, and general location for optimal connection.

Community Building:

Conducted research on the target audience, which included gamers seeking to connect with others who share similar interests and availability for game sessions. This involved understanding the motivations and challenges gamers face when looking for new players, such as language barriers, age differences, and time zone disparities.

Final Outcome

The final outcome was a fully functional web application prototype called Game Together. The app successfully provided a platform for gamers to form groups, add players, and communicate via chat, facilitating collaboration and community building. Key features included the ability to create and share favorite game lists, search for gamers based on specific games and platforms, and view detailed profiles of potential gaming partners.

Despite being my first venture into backend development and working under time constraints, I maintained a detailed Wiki page to provide comprehensive information about the app's features and functionality. This project not only enhanced my backend development skills but also deepened my understanding of user experience and community building in the gaming industry.
