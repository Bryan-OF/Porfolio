Website concept: Watch Party

Description

  • Bryan Ortiz Franco

  • 17 November, 2022

This is my streaming website/app concept: Watch Party. My concept for Watch Party was a streaming app that would bring all other streaming services to one place, making it easier for the user to search for content. It would also allow you to watch content together with friends and family members that are not living in the same household.
