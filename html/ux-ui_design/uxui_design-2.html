Website concept: Watch Party

In the "Responsive Multi-Device Design" project, I designed an streaming website/app concept called Watch Party. The idea for this app is that users can watch videos and series with friends and family through one app. The app would work across multiple platforms, such as smartphones and smart TVs, allowing users to watch at their convenience at the same time without dalay. It would also allow you to watch content together with friends and family members that are not living in the same household. This project focused on reseacrhing and design application that would give users.

Project objective

The goal was to provide an innovative solution for collaborative video viewing that focused on ease of use and accessibility. This project was perfect for me because it challenged both my design and development skills and gave me the opportunity to create a product that addresses a real need.

User scenario

The primary user for this application would be a student in their twenties who watches a lot of TV series/movies together. Since everyone has a different schedule, they don't have time to watch together. With Watch Party, they could watch simultaneously on different devices, such as smartphones and smart TVs, and communicate via video and text chat, to watch together. This solves the problem of physical distance and different schedules and also synchronises content to play at the same time.

Product requirements

Watch Party needs the following requirements to work perfectly:

Must have

• A calling system for video and audio calls.

• A notification system to inform users when a Watch Party starts.

• A messaging system for text chat.

• A security system to protect accounts.

• A calendar system to schedule Watch Parties.

• A search function to find videos and series on various streaming platforms.

Nice to have

• Host control over Watch Party access.

• A voting poll system to decide which series or film to watch.

• A To-Watch calendar to save future viewing options.

Design process

The design phase began by researching existing products such as Discord, Teleparty and YouTube to see which would be best to integrate the best features into the app. Inspiration was drawn from various streaming websites and the final design combined elements of Netflix, Discord and Outlook. Using wireflows and prototypes, the app was iteratively improved. This would also give users a sense of familiarity and it would also make the app easier to control.

The End result

The end result was a user-friendly and versatile application that allowed videos to be viewed collectively. The combination of different designs ensured easy navigation and an intuitive user experience. Although improvements can still be made, the app provides a solid foundation for further development and refinement.

This project not only improved my design skills, but also my ability to design and implement user-centric solutions.
