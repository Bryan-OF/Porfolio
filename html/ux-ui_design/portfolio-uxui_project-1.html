Website concept: Lekker Bakkie

To convey our website idea for Lekker Bakkie, a coffee delivery service, I created this prototype website with the help of my group. Our concept for Lekker Bakkie is a coffee delivery service that delivers coffee around the city daily using cargo bikes. It is designed to assist people who are running late for work or cannot visit a coffee shop. Customers can order their drinks in advance and pick them up without having to wait for their preparation.

Design Challenge

In a bustling city like Amsterdam, many professionals face a common problem: they are often pressed for time and cannot afford to wait in long lines for their daily coffee. Whether they are running late for work or trying to maximize their short breaks, the inconvenience of obtaining a quality cup of coffee can disrupt their routines and reduce productivity. The challenge is to create a service that delivers high-quality coffee quickly and conveniently to these busy individuals, without them having to visit a coffee shop.

Design Process

Our design process began with thorough market research and user analysis to understand the pain points of our target audience.
We identified key issues such as time constraints, the need for quality coffee, and the inconvenience of traditional coffee shop visits.

1. User Research: Conducted surveys and interviews with working professionals to gather insights into their daily routines and coffee consumption habits.

2. Persona Development: Created detailed personas to represent our primary user groups, focusing on their specific needs and challenges.

3. Ideation: Brainstormed various solutions, including mobile apps, pre-order systems, and different delivery methods.

4. Prototyping: Developed a prototype for a user-friendly app that allows customers to order coffee in advance and select a delivery location.

5. Testing: Tested the prototype with a small group of users, gathering feedback to refine the service and improve the user experience.

Solution

The result of our development process is Lekker Bakkie, an innovative coffee delivery service that addresses the time constraints of busy professionals in Amsterdam.

• System: Customers can easily order their favorite coffee via our app or website. They can specify the type of coffee, time, and delivery location.

• Efficient Delivery: Using cargo bikes, our couriers navigate through the city quickly, ensuring that the coffee is delivered fresh and on time.

• Quality Assurance: We partner with top-quality coffee suppliers and hire skilled baristas to ensure every cup meets the highest standards.

Conclusion

Lekker Bakkie transforms the way busy professionals in Amsterdam get their coffee. By focusing on convenience, speed, and quality, we provide a seamless solution that integrates into their hectic schedules. This design challenge has not only led to an innovative service but also promises to enhance the daily lives of our customers by ensuring they never have to compromise on their coffee experience.
