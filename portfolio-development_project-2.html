Amsterdam Go App prototype

Description

  • Bryan Ortiz Franco

  • 11 November, 2021

  • Individual interversion project

Embark on a journey of discovery through the city of Amsterdam with Amsterdam Go. This entertaining app serves as your personal guide, showing your location and directing you to the city's most sought-after cultural attractions and hotspots. Travel by public transport or by bike, the choice is yours! Make memories and stand out from the crowd by collecting digital stickers, taking photos and competing with friends to see who can explore Amsterdam the most.

Technology

  • CSS
